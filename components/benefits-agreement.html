Benefits Service Agreement

This Service Agreement is entered into as of [date] between Ziero Financial Inc., a Delaware company with a place of business at 739 Bryant Street, Suite 207, San Francisco, CA 94107 (“Ziero”), and Employer (“Company”, “you”).

Witnesseth:

WHEREAS, Ziero provides an employee benefit program enabling qualified employees with appropriate credit scores to obtain zero-interest loans at specified sums; and

WHEREAS, Company wishes to engage Ziero to provide this benefit to its employees on the terms set forth herein, and is willing to pay Ziero as set forth herein for administration and maintenance of the benefit program;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises set forth herein, the parties hereby agree as follows:

1. Service.

1.1 Ziero will provide service to Company, in the form of an internet portal, explanatory information, and discretionary support, for employees who wish to enroll in Ziero’s employee benefit loan program.

1.2 The service will consist of an Internet portal, anticipated to be www.zierofinance.com, which qualifying employees may utilize to apply for, and should they qualify, to obtain loans on the terms specified by Ziero. In addition, Ziero will provide Company with instructions, introductory and explanatory documentation in its discretion, for relaying to Company’s employees and for the Company’s informational purposes. Ziero will further provide Company with additional support in its discretion as it deems appropriate with respect to implementation and operation of the benefit program for Company.

1.3 Ziero will retain full discretion as to whether or not a particular employee qualifies for a Ziero loan, subject to all applicable legal requirements. Ziero anticipates that qualified employees will be limited to those with credit scores equal to or in excess of 640 who are employed on a part or full time basis by Company with no expectation of near term separation. Ziero will have no liability to Company for refusing to extend any specific loan product to any particular employee.

1.4 Ziero will notify Company of each loan consummated with a Company employee, without necessarily identifying employee, as well as whether any employees no longer have outstanding balances on any Ziero loans previously obtained.

2. Company Cooperation, Support, and Understanding.

2.1 Company will provide Ziero with all information reasonable and necessary for provision of such Services. Company will be granted access to the Ziero platform for the purposes of entering employees who are eligible for consideration for loans on the Ziero platform, and will be responsible for ensuring such data are correct.

2.3 Company shall upload information only of part time, full time, indefinite-term employees (not including contractors, , retirees, temporary employees, or other employees that Company expects to terminate in the near future), whom Company deems potentially eligible to apply for Ziero loans according to its own additional eligibility parameters. Company may base these additional eligibility parameters on any legally permissible criteria it sees fit. In the event that Company receives notice that a particular employee is terminating or intends to terminate her or his employment, Company will remove this employee from the database. Company will bear full responsibility for ensuring that the data it uploads concerning its employees and their eligibility for Ziero benefits are accurate and current.

3. Payment.

3.1 In consideration of Ziero’s performance of the service, Company will pay to Ziero an administrative fee in accordance with the pricing plan selected by Company.

3.2 Ziero will invoice Company for administrative fees upon the approval of a loan, prior to disbursement of funds by Ziero to employee/borrower. Payment of the administrative fee will be due and payable before proceeds of such loan are disbursed to employee/borrower.

4. Limitation of Obligations

4.1 Ziero’s obligations are limited to the provision of Service as set forth herein. Ziero shall have no obligation to provide additional products and services of any kind, nor shall Ziero be obligated to extend any loans that it does not in its sole discretion deem appropriate.

Company shall not attempt to reverse engineer, decompile or otherwise replicate any Ziero services or materials. Company shall keep this Agreement, as well as any information designated by Ziero, as proprietary, confidential and will not disclose such information to any third parties (other than Company employees) without Ziero’s express written consent.
