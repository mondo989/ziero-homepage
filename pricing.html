How are the loans 0% and what is the fee for?

When an employee opts to access the Ziero loan perk by submitting an application on our website, our finance partners (e.g., community banks) provide us the capital so Ziero can issue the interest-free loan.

At that point, Ziero changes a small fee to the employer. The fee covers the cost of the transaction. Ziero’s platform does the heavy lifting, making it possible for banks to lend, using our no-interest model, to those in need.

Our portal makes it easy for any company or non-profit to provide some financial breathing room to their employees.

It’s a win-win situation that improves the financial wellness of employees everywhere.
