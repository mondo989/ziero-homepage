Other Terms

Unsuccessful payment fee

When an automatic payment fails and is rejected by the borrower's bank, an unsuccessful payment fee is charged to the borrower to cover the cost Ziero Financial incurs on the transaction. This could happen if there is insufficient balance in the borrower's bank account, the bank account has been closed or if the account is otherwise suspended. Each attempt to collect the monthly payment is considered a transaction, so an unsuccessful payment fee may be assessed for each failed attempt. The amount of an unsuccessful payment fee is $15.

Late payment fee

When a borrower's payment is late after a 10-day grace period, a late payment fee is assessed on the 11th day for the delay. The amount of a late payment fee is $10. This fee may be charged only once per late payment.

Check processing fee

If a borrower elects to make payments via check, there will be a $15 processing fee per payment. There is no additional charge if the borrower makes payments via direct debit from their bank account (ACH).

Prepayment fee

You can prepay anytime – there are no prepayment penalties or fees.
