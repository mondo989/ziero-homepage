How the Ziero Program Work


System Setup

  • Customize employee loan portal
  • Setup bank account for funding
  • Payroll integration setup (if requested)





Employee Loan Application


  • Employees can redeem their loan benefit online at any time via a 5 minute application.
  • Ziero verifies all applicants with HR system to confirm eligibility and pre-determined loan terms.




Loan Servicing

  • Loan repayment can be deducted via payroll or bank ACH. We report successful repayment to the credit bureau to help your employees improve their personal credit scores.
  • If an employee departs the company. Ziero continues to collect from borrower’s bank account until the loan is repaid.
